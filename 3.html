Today the world got lucky,
It gained your light, your grace

Born with hands those heal,
And a heart that no pain could chase

In the rush of life and the noise of the day,
I often forget the little things I should say.

But even when the skies turn grey,
My love for you does not delay.

I fumble often, miss your cues,
But never would I choose to lose

I failed to listen, I failed to see,
But in my heart, you mean everything to me.

I miss all those chats or midnight calls,
And the warmth when the silence falls.

So on this day, Oh my dearest one,
Know that my heart has just begun

To learn, to grow, to understand
To hold your hand within my hand.

On your special day, I vow to grow,
To understand more, to love you and show,

Forgive my flaws, the times I fall,
I’m here, I’m yours, despite it all.

That even when we fight, or when we fall,
You are my heart, my world, my all.

No matter what you are my northern star
Happiest Birthday again, my guiding star..
